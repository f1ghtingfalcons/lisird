About LISIRD

Welcome to the LASP Interactive Solar IRradiance Data Center (LISIRD) website. We provide access to a comprehensive set of solar spectral irradiance measurements from the soft X-ray (XUV) at 0.1 nm up to the near infrared (NIR) at 2700 nm, as well as state-of-the-art measurements of Total Solar Irradiance (TSI). We also provide an extensive set of solar irradiance models and historical solar irradiance reconstructions generated by LASP researchers.


This web application is dynamically created using the metadata tools developed by the LASP Extended Metadata Repository(LEMR) team and VIVO. Updates to metadata should be done through the LASP internal VIVO instance. Questions or comments can be sent to: email here.


Currently, we provide data for several LASP missions:

SME - Solar Mesosphere Explorer

SORCE - Solar Radiation and Climate Experiment

TIMED/SEE - Thermosphere Ionosphere Mesosphere Energetics and Dynamics

UARS/SOLSTICE - Upper Atmosphere Research Satellite


Coming soon:

SDO - Solar Dynamics Observatory

SNOE - Student Nitric Oxide Explorer


For more information on other LASP missions, please visit the LASP mission page

LISIRD: LASP Interactive Solar Irradiance Data Center

Composite Time Series Solar Models Solar Spectral Irradiance Total Solar Irradiance

In this data center, the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP) provides a variety of solar irradiance data sets, including daily measurements, reference spectra, composite time series, and model results. Available wavelengths range from 0.1 nm to 2.4 microns, although not all wavelengths are covered continuously.
